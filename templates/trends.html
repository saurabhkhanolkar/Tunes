About:

1) PyTunes is a Music recommendation engine.

2) PyTunes studies your musical taste and recommends some of the global trending songs you might love!

3) Interested to know about the Data Engineering and Data Mining that went into this project? click here

First Visit? See how to use:

1) Open Spotify app or go to https://open.spotify.com/
2) Copy the url to your favourite playlist or album, and paste!
3) Click on Recommend and enjoy the recommendations !

...And if you just want to test it, choose any pre-specified options from the dropdown.
